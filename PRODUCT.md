# 100 AI Product Context

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

100 AI is a public archive of small AI product experiments built through vibe coding. The site should make the work feel cumulative: a long-running practice, not a one-off landing page.

## Users

- Visitors who want to understand what has already been shipped.
- Potential collaborators, customers, or peers who want a fast read on the builder's taste and execution.
- The owner, who needs a simple public index that can grow from 2 projects to 100.

## Brand Voice

Quietly obsessive, experimental, concrete. It should feel like an inventor's bench with working objects, not a generic AI startup page.

## Strategic Principles

- Show progress toward 100 without turning the page into a vanity metric.
- Let shipped products feel inspectable and trustworthy.
- Keep upcoming slots visible so the project reads as an ongoing challenge.
- Prefer durable structure over decorative effects.

## Anti-References

- Generic neon cyber grids.
- SaaS hero with oversized metric cards.
- Repeated icon-card feature grids.
- Overexplained copy that says "AI powered" without showing what was built.
