# 100 AI Design Context

## Direction

The site uses a dark workshop surface with restrained, high-contrast project tiles. The physical scene is a late-night desk with a matte green cutting mat, brass labels, and a few glowing monitor edges: focused, not arcade-like.

## Color

- Strategy: committed dark surface with a small warm accent and sparse cyan operational cues.
- Use OKLCH colors in new CSS.
- Avoid pure black and pure white.
- Accent color should mark progress, links, and live project states.

## Typography

- Sora carries the brand and interface.
- JetBrains Mono is used only for numbers, short status labels, and technical tags.
- Chinese copy should be concise, with generous line height on dark backgrounds.

## Components

- Header: compact fixed navigation with visible project count and contact action.
- Hero: project statement, completion rhythm, and a progress track.
- Project cards: clear distinction between shipped and planned slots. Shipped cards should expose name, description, tags, and visit affordance.
- Contact dialog: simple, accessible, and easy to dismiss.

## Motion

Subtle state motion only: hover, focus, and dialog reveal. No decorative scanning effects or page-load choreography.
